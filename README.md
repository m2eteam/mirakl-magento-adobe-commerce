# Mirakl Magento (Adobe Commerce) Connect by M2E

Mirakl Magento (Adobe Commerce) Connect by M2E is a trusted Magento extension that enables merchants of all sizes to fully integrate their Magento-based systems with the Mirakl marketplace.

The extension makes it easy to upload your Magento inventory to Mirakl. It provides automatic inventory and order synchronization, keeping your product and order information up-to-date on Magento and Mirakl.

Here's how else Mirakl Magento Connect by M2E can benefit your business:

- List your Magento products on Mirakl in several clicks
- Customize your Magento product settings specifically for Mirakl
- Enjoy real-time, automated synchronization for prices and quantities
- Manage Mirakl orders directly in Magento

Check out our [user documentation](https://docs-m2.m2epro.com/docs-category/mirakl-magento-adobe-commerce-integration/) to learn more about the extension.
